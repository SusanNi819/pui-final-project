CAPRICORN

Capricornus is a zodiacal constellation lying in the southern sky between Aquarius and Sagittarius, at about 21 hours right ascension and 20° south declination.

In astrology, Capricorn is the 10th sign of the zodiac, considered as governing the period from about December 22 to about January 19.

One explanation of the fishtail with which the goat is often represented is found in the Greek myth of Pan, who, to avoid the monster Typhon, jumped into the water just as he was changing into animal shape.
