A horoscope is a diagram of the relative positions of planets and signs of the zodiac for use by astrologers in inferring individual character and personality traits and in foretelling events of a person's life.

Zodiac signs are the twelve 30 degree sectors that make up Earth's 360-degree orbit around the Sun.

Flip the cards to discover the signs.
