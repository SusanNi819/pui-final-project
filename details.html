LEO

Leo is a zodiacal constellation lying in the northern sky between Cancer and Virgo, at about 10 hours 30 minutes right ascension and 15° north declination.

In astrology, Leo is the fifth sign of the zodiac, considered as governing the period from about July 23 to about August 22.

Its representation as a lion is usually linked with the Nemean lion slain by Heracles.

Element: Fire

Ruling Planet: Sun

Birth Stone: Peridot

Zodiac Symbol: Lion

Best Compatible Signs With Leos:

Aries - Gemini - Libra

Least Compatible Signs With Leos:

Cancer - Pisces - Scorpio
